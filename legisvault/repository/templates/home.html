The Sangguniang Bayan, as mandated by the Local Government Code of 1991 (RA 7160), serves as the legislative body of the municipality. It is responsible for enacting ordinances and approving resolutions that promote good governance and community welfare. The council exercises fiscal authority by reviewing and approving the municipal budget, allocating funds for local programs, and authorizing financial transactions. It also oversees development planning by adopting land use and zoning ordinances and municipal development plans. In addition, the Sangguniang Bayan regulates businesses, public utilities, and community activities, while ensuring environmental protection and sustainable use of resources. Above all, it plays a crucial role in delivering essential services such as health, education, public works, and social welfare to enhance the quality of life for all constituents.
