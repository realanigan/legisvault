Sangguniang Bayan Publications

A Sangguniang Bayan (SB) Publication serves as the official medium through which the municipal council informs the public of its enacted resolutions, ordinances, and other legislative measures. It ensures transparency, accessibility, and accountability by making local laws and policies available to constituents. Typically, the publication contains the full texts of approved ordinances and resolutions, accompanied by explanatory notes or summaries to aid public understanding. Beyond its legal purpose of compliance with the Local Government Code’s mandate on publication for effectivity, the SB publication also functions as a valuable record of the municipality’s legislative history, enabling citizens, researchers, and government offices to track policy directions and community priorities over time.
